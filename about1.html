Our Founder

Jai Sathguru Fine Arts

It is a new sabha in PORUR founded by Shri. A V MANIKANTAAN (Founder) especially to promote music and culture.

Shri. AVM has a thought to create platform for artiste all over the world. The talented and deserved artist should get a stage for their performance without any obligation.

Shri.AVM himself is an established mridangam artist wish to teach rhythm to many young boys and girls and impart the knowledge of rhythm / music to all.

With this desire he started a music school named JAI SATHGURU SANGEETHA VIDYALAYA at Adambakkam in the year 2005, with only 10 students.

Now the school has grown with 100â€™s of students and have branches all over South India.

With the support of students, well-wishers and music connoisseur he began this new sabha JAI SATHGURU FINE ARTS in the locality of Madanandapuram, Porur.

This sabha was started three months back in a small premises with the capacity of 30/40 people can sit where they conducted chamber concerts, LecDems, competitions, practice sessions and so on.

Seeing the response now the Sabha wish to expand their wings for the general public.

With that mark the first public concert was held in Sri Durga Lakshmi Saraswati Temple, Santhosh nagar, Madanandapuram, Chennai and the concert was sang by Smt, Gayathri Venkatragavan who is a star musician with great humility and it was supported by the stalwart M.R Gopinath on the violin, Shri. A V Manikantaan himself played the mridangam and on the ghatam Sri Sainath.

The audience of the locality came in huge numbers and they stay throughout the concert.

About the founder - A V Manikantaan

A V Manikantaan was born in Chennai and hails from a musical family whose maternal grandfather Sri . S Gopalakrishnan , was a mridangam artist and his mother G Subbulakshmi is a music teacher and a violinist, His brother Chennai A Srikanth Bhagavathar is also a renowned artist in the field of Namasankeerthanam.

A V Manikantaan began learning the art under Late Shri. Vaideeswaran Koil Vaidhyanatha Iyer from the year 1988-1996.

After 1997, he had the privilege and boon to continue his musical training under the divine tutelage of Shri Guru Kaaraikkudi Mani.

He as completed M A Rhythmology ,Indian Music through The University of Madras in the year 2008 He is an "A" Graded artist from All India Radio and Obtained the Award of Scholarship under the Cultural Talent Search Scholarship Scheme given by CCRT , New Delhi Founder of JAI SATHGURU SANGEETHA VIDYALAYA (Music School) and teaching Mridangam and Konnakol for more than 75 students. Having a vision to bring more number of younger generation into the Mridangam field.

Worked as a Guest Faculty ( Mridangam & Konnakol ) for Post Graduate Students in Dr. J Jayalalithaa Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts University.

He had won the Best Mridangist award from The Music Academy Chennai in 2006 and 2011 , won the Best accompaniment award for the year 2002 and 2008 at Sri Krishna Gana Sabha , Best Percussionist Artist award in 2014 "Guru Karaikudi Mani " award from Karthik Fine Arts , Chennai

In his musical journey , he is accompanying many stalwarts and great vidwans like Sri. O S Thiyagarajan , Smt .Sudha Ragunathan , Sri Kunakudi Balamuralikrishna and Special Performance with Legends like Padmasri HARIDWARAMANGALAM SRI K. PALANIVEL , Ghatam maestro SRI VIKKU VINAYAKRAM , his guru " GURU KAARAIKKUDI MANI" and he is presently giving mridangam accompaniment to various star-studded artist, few to name are Sri.Rajesh Vaidhya, Madolin Sri.U Rajesh, Smt.SuchithraBalasubramanian, Sri.R.P.Shravan ,Smt.Vasudha Ravi, Smt.Vidya Kalyanaraman ,Smt. In Namasankeerthanam field he has accompanied, across generations, almost all the renowned and leading Bhagavathars like HH Sri RAMANANDA SRASWATI SWAMIJI, Udayalur Sri KALYANARAMAN Bhagavathar, Sri OS SUNDAR Bhagavathar, Sri Erode RAJAMANI Bhagavathar, Shri VIJAY and BALAJI Bhagavathar, Kadaiyanallur Sri RAJAGOPAL Bhagavathar, Shri Srikanth kountanian Bhagavathar Shri Gnanaeshwar Bhagavathar, New Jersey Shri Swaminathan Bhagavathar and so on The Duo brothers Chennai A Srikanth Bhagavathar and A V Manikantaan are currently creating an impact in the field.
